Did You Know???

All credit goes to authors of "Ready, Set, Squash!". Go check out this awesome instructional book to learn more!

  • Did you know that there was a squash court on the Titanic?.
  • Squash was voted the healthiest sport in the world, according to Forbes Magazine.
  • Did you know that you can burn between 500 and 1000 calories in just one squash match?
  • Jahangir Khan spent 72 consecutive months as the men's squash world #1 compared to tennis's Roger Federer at 59 months. In fact, her won 555 matches in a row, establishing the longest winning streak in the history of professional sports!
  • Australia's Cameron Pilley has the fasted recorded ball speed in history at 175 miles per hour.
  • Malaysia's Nicol David was women's world #1 for nearly a decade!
